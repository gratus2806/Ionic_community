Welcome To Chanasma



Our Jain community of Chanasma was originally located in North Gujarat, India. The Community is now spread all over India and Overseas, but majority members are located at Mumbai, Surat and Ahmedabad. With blessings of "Shree Bhatewa Parshwanth Bhagwan" members of the community have progressed in all aspects of life especially in Religious, Business & Education.
